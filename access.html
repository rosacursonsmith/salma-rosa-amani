Access London

Making London Just that Little Bit Easier

Access London is a site to help people with disabilities and limited mobility get about more easily. If you go to our travel page you can find if stations are accessible or not, alongside other resources to aid with travel. If you have any updates which affect accessible travel (eg lift closures, building works etc), then please post it on our forum so that everyone can keep up to date. Happy travels!
