Our website is here to provide up to date information on accessibility at TfL train stations With only 25% of the London Tube stations being wheelchair accessible, there is a need for as much information about accessible locations. During 2019, there has been a 5% increase in accessible trains; this lack of accessibility causes many to use other forms of transport. For those with limited mobility who utilise public transport, find all the information you will need, and up to date announcements at train stations all over London. The forum page is available for discussions and real time information about wheelchair access at train stations in London. Useful for train strikes, delayed trains, unplanned closures etc. Stay happy and keep travelling!
